Culture

Binnenhof

Address

Binnenhof, 2513 AA Den Haag

Opening hours

10:00 - 17:00

The Binnenhof is the political center of the Netherlands and houses, among other things, the Senate and House of Representatives of the States General.

The complex, which dates back to the 13th century, is one of the oldest parliament buildings still in use in the world. During Heritage Day, you will have access to normally closed areas.

Accessibility

Wheelchair accessible on the ground floor. Elevator available.

Extra informatie

Free tours every hour. Last tour at 4:00 PM.
